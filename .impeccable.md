# Impeccable Design Context — Kiefer's Little World

## Design Context

### Users
Fans of Kiefer (jazz-piano / hip-hop beatmaker) discovering his discography playfully.
They arrive from a link, probably on desktop, curious and relaxed. The job to be done:
wander a tiny world, stumble on albums, and listen — zero friction, zero instructions
beyond "use the arrows". Session length: 2–10 minutes of chill exploration.

### Brand Personality
**Dreamlike, childlike, warm.**
The music is chill hip-hop beats with jazz piano on top — mellow, nostalgic, a little
whimsical, never aggressive. The game should feel like a picture book you can walk
through: soft, hand-made, slightly wobbly, full of small delights. Emotions to evoke:
calm, wonder, a smile.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Reference material = the 9 album covers themselves.** Each zone borrows its palette
  and props directly from its cover (dog + red toy piano; knit stripes; giant piano keys;
  retro CRT in a night garden; superbloom hills; cozy amber room; painted porch memory;
  sunset bubble dreamscape; yellow LA bungalow).
- Overall world: soft cream/pastel daylight sky drifting into themed pockets; hand-drawn
  wobbly outlines, rounded everything, crayon/gouache texture feel; miniature/diorama scale.
- Typography: childlike handwriting (e.g. Gaegu / Patrick Hand / Schoolbell), lowercase.
- **Anti-references (never):** cyan-on-black "gamer" UI, purple gradient AI-slop,
  glassmorphism, neon, Inter/Roboto default look, harsh drop shadows, corporate polish.
- Light, dreamy world; UI chrome (mini player bar) is a small soft black bar — the one
  dark element, like a piano key.

### Design Principles
1. **The covers are the art director.** Every zone's colors and props come from its album
   cover, miniaturized and hand-drawn — recognition should be instant for fans.
2. **Childlike, not childish.** Wobbly lines and toy scale, but composed with real care:
   generous whitespace, gentle rhythm, nothing cluttered.
3. **Music first, UI invisible.** The Spotify/YouTube machinery is hidden; all the player
   sees is a tiny black music bar and a friendly "press enter to listen" bubble.
4. **Small delights everywhere.** Props react when approached (dog looks up, bunny hops,
   flowers bloom underfoot). Micro-interactions over menus.
5. **Calm motion.** Everything eases and floats; no flashing, no urgency. Respect
   prefers-reduced-motion.

### Tech Notes
- Single-page canvas game, arrow-key movement, Enter to play the zone's track.
- Audio via hidden YouTube IFrame API player (full tracks, preloaded/cued per zone).
- Track map lives in one config object: album name → YouTube video id → zone theme.
